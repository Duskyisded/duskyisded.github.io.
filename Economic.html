The production of rice, a basic material for food security, is increasing. By 2003, Palay's annual production was 437,000 tons, 33.5 times more than two years ago. As a result, the state was able to significantly increase its sufficiency from 65% to over 84%. This improvement can be attributed to the introduction and promotion of hybrid rice, which increased rice yields to 3.8 tonnes per hectare. The success of the program has almost quintupled the area of hybrid rice. The highest hybrid yield was recorded at 10.3 tons per hectare.
